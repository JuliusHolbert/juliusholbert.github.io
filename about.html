About Me

My name is Julius, and I am a full stack/mobile developer who builds robust services and full stack applications! My combination of time building robust and complex projects has taught me the skills I need to confidently develop software solutions with very minimal oversight. I have built applications across the stack taking concepts to finished products in a variety of languages and frameworks. My strength lies in being a systems-minded developer that is self-driven, and comfortable with contrasting different implementation strategies before writing any code.
